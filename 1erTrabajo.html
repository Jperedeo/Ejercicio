Horacio Pagani and the beginnings

He opened a small shop where he worked at a very young age, gaining valuable experience in craftsmanship. By the age of 20, Pagani had designed and built his first F3 racer. The turning point in Pagani's life came when he was hired by Renault to improve the body of a racing car.
